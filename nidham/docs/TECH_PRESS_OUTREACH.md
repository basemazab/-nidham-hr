NIDHAM HR LAUNCHES EGYPT-FIRST HR + PAYROLL + CRM PLATFORM
EGP-Priced Alternative to Gulf HR Software Aims to Digitalize 5 Million SMBs

DAMIETTA, EGYPT — May 2026 — Nidham HR (نِظام) today announced the launch of Egypt's first fully-integrated HR, Payroll, and CRM software platform built specifically for compliance with Egyptian Labor Law 12/2003 and Social Insurance Law 148/2019.

While Gulf-based HR software (Bayzat, ZenHR) dominates Egypt's enterprise market, smaller SMBs — over 5 million businesses representing 75% of Egypt's private employment — remain locked out due to USD pricing and lack of Egyptian-law support. Nidham HR addresses this with EGP-denominated pricing starting at 749 EGP per month (~$15) — 70% cheaper than Gulf alternatives.

KEY DIFFERENTIATORS

• **Native Egyptian Compliance:** Automated payroll calculations using the exact 2026 NOSI insurance brackets, the new Law 175/2023 tax schedule, and Egyptian-specific calendar logic (÷26 days vs ÷30).

• **Official Form Generation:** Direct PDF generation of Social Insurance Forms 1, 2, and 6, plus experience certificates — replacing manual typing that costs HR teams 30-50 minutes per employee.

• **WhatsApp Bot for Employees:** First HR platform in MENA to offer native WhatsApp interaction. Employees request leave, check balance, receive payslips, and access certificates via WhatsApp — eliminating email-dependence that has historically blocked digital HR adoption in Egypt.

• **GPS Attendance with Face Match:** Designed for field workers (drivers, sales reps, contractors), the platform combines geofence-based check-in with selfie face verification, fully PDPL-compliant.

• **Legally-Binding E-Signature:** Built-in advanced e-signature module recognized under Law 15/2004.

• **Integrated CRM:** Sales pipeline, contact management, and WhatsApp-based customer interactions in a single platform.

We built Nidham to make professional HR accessible at EGP prices that make sense for the Egyptian SMB economy."
